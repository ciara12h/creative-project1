About Kiirsi

Kiirsi Branham grew up in northern Wisconsin, where her family, her animals, her favorite books, and the northwoods forests were her best friends. She received degrees from LDS Business College and a B. A. in English from Brigham Young University. She makes her home in northern Utah with her husband, three children, and two dogs. Her days are filled with family, pets, music, books, writing, and yarn.

Kiirsi is currently working on the first book in her series, "The Crochet Club", a middle-grade fantasy book, but has many future projects and ideas that she is excited to begin working on.
